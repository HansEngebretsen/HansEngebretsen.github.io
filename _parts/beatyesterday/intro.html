Garmin

Beat Yesterday Campaign

One of Garmin's first fitness campaigns to promote its wearable devices.

Visit the Site ➚

About

'Beat Yesterday' was a simple challenge to viewers across all fitness and activity levels: better yourself, one day at a time. The digital component was a part to the a larger Beat Yesterday campaign consisting of print, social, video, etc.

Role

Working alongside Ryan Glendening who served as Creative Director, I helped design and develop the interactive/digital experience which included an interactive website, branding style guide, as well as email campaign.
