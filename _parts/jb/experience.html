The experience

Views, pages, experiences

Developing and designing the experience for a fully functioning application can be quite challenging, particularly when it comes to communication. Pages, views, states, hovers, pop-ups, overlays all mush together and are difficult to represent. Using visual user flows, we mapped out all of the pages and views at the start and used this as our frame of reference for the rest of the project.
