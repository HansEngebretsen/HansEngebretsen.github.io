About

More than just an advertising campaign, Probably Wear This was a digital effort to help solve increased user confusion over Garmin's product line. It was also a follow-up, or contunuation of the Beat Yesterday campaign.

Role

I served as Art Director on the campaign and led the conception, design, and developement of ProbablyWearThis.com. I collaborated alongside Vanessa Righeimer (Copywriter) Ryan Glendening (Creative Director). Work included photography, interactive website, digital ads, and social advertising.
