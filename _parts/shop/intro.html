About

Garmin.com is a giant ecommerce site that gets over 300 million hits a year, is the one of the top 1,000 most visited sites in the world, the top 5 consumer electronics sites globally, and generates millions of dollars of revenue a year. Redesigning the site was as difficult, politically charged, involved, and thrilling as you can imagine.

Role

Operating as a team of three, Chris Partise, Katie Heinie and I led the design while working alongside UX teams, business marketing clients, and IT implementation teams for over a year. As you can imagine the redesign and restructure of such a large site involves the work of several teams.
