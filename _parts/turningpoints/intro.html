About

The Garmin Turning points campaign focused on stories from 7 elite athletes. Each story highlighted the story of a specific 'turning point' in the athletes career, a sort of 'do or die' moment. The site was launched in tandem with the release of Garmin's Forerunner 735xt, a watch designed for elite runners & triathletes.

Role

Alongside Tommy Short, Mellissa Mulligan, and Rebecca Sommers (who helped with video & media for the project), I led the design, development and UX for this project.
