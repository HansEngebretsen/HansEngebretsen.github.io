About

Garmin VIRB is a line of action cameras focused on professional niche markets. It leverages advanced data integration and rugged design to give an exciting look at any situation.

Role

Working with the awesome web team at Garmin composed of UX researchers, IAs , copywriters, and developers over the course of 5 months, I served as lead designer and front-end developer on the project.

The experience

Focusing on experience

User Experience

Through 3 rounds of user tests, 3 prototypes, and countless revisions, we worked to determine content heirarchy that ensured users could seamlessly click and tap through the site.

Site Map

Instead of a typical landing page, this project involved full ecommerce functionality as well as fitting activities, products, shopping, grid view, home pages, all into a structure that made sense to a consumer.

Color Color Color!

Color has never been "my jam" as a designer, and I'm not nearly as good at utilizing it as other designers (in fact, I consider it one of my weaker points).
