Moodboards

What does it make you feel?

Looking for a challenge? Try getting 30 people in a large corporation to agree on a design direction for a flagship project. VIRB didn't have any sort of campaign to attach to, so I had the fantastic opportunity to create my own art direciton/look & feel for the site. We kicked off the process by using moodboards to narrow down the experience to something that felt actively technical, immersive, and expansive
