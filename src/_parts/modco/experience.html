The experience

Views, pages, experiences

Developing and designing the experience for a fully functioning application, can be challenging when it comes to communication. Pages, views, states, hovers, popups, overlays can all mush together and be confusing shown as images. We approached this by mapping out all of the pages and views of most user flows, and using that as our central place to work from.
