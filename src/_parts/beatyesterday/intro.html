Garmin

Beat Yesterday Campaign

A startup app that saught to simplify smart RSS/article reading.

About

JournalBot was a smart RSS feed / article aggregation app that learned and adapted to your RSS preferences. The back end services and algorithms had been created, but the app needed a vision for the customer experience, visual brand, and front-end code.

Role

I had a fantastic time collaborating with one of the best web designers I know, Chris Partise on this project. We worked together on UX, IA, and site structure. While Chris focused on visual design and brand development, I focused on front-end developement and interaction design.
